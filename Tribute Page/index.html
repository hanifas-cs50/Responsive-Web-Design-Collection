Kevin Macleod's career:

  • Music distribution
    • MacLeod states that he releases his music under Creative Commons licenses to maximize the number of people who can use his music.
    • MacLeod's most popular license is the Creative Commons Attribution copyright license; his music is free to use but must be credited.
  • Documentary MacLeod is the subject of a documentary film titled Royalty Free: The Music of Kevin MacLeod.
  • Awards and accolades In 2015, MacLeod was awarded the International Honorary Web Video Award at the 2015 German Web Video Awards by the European Web Video Academy for his lifetime achievement in influencing the German web video community.
